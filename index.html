Banshee's Haven is located on a secluded and private costal area with the best of the wild atlantic at our doorstep. Ideal for anyone looking to escape the city stress and connect with nature. With only six rooms and maximum of twelve guests, privacy and full attention from our staff is guaranteed.

All our rooms have stunning views, are decorated with comfort in mind and comes with a choice of Continental or Full Irish breakfast. We also have a small but delicious dinner offering paid separetly. The menu vary with one vegan and one omnivore each day, but we strive to cater to all dietary needs so please let us know of your needs.
